About Us

Come Be a Member!

We are a group of snowboarders that like to just go and have fun! We are located in the University of Calgary and we welcome everyone in all skill groups! We wish to spread the culture of snowboarding with anyone who is interested and we have been here for a very long time. If you are interested in joining, or have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us below, or you can come visit us in our club room! We look forward to seeing you here and hope that you are able to join us in our events, or even just come to chill!
